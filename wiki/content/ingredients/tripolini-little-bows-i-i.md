tripolini = little bows  tri-pah-LEE-nee  Some pasta producers use the name tripolini to describe tiny bows which commemorate the Italian conquest of Tripoli.  Some use it to describe long ribbons that are similar to fettuccine, but ruffled along one edge.  stelle OR ditalini OR farfalline OR other soup pasta
