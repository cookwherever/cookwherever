Tolosana bean = Spanish Tolosana bean = Prince bean These beans are especially good in seafood dishes. Substitutes: red kidney beans OR azuki beans tongues of fire bean Equivalents: One cup dried beans = 3 cups cooked beans. Notes: This bean is a close relative of the cranberry bean. Despite its name, it's not hot at all. Substitutes: cranberry beans trout bean = Jacob's cattle bean = forellen bean Notes: This German heirloom bean is relatively sweet. It's especially good in soups and casseroles.
