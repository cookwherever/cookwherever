Tomme Crayeuse cheese  TUM cray-YOUZ This soft French cheese is rich and buttery.  Don't eat the rind.    Tomme de Savoie OR Saint Nectaire OR Muenster Tomme de Savoie cheese = tomme de montagne    TUM de sah-VWAH    This is a mild and pleasant French cheese that's semi-soft when young, firmer when aged.   Substitutes:  Tomme Crayeuse OR Saint Nectaire OR Muenster
