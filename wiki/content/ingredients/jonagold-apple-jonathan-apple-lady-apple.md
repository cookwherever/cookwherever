Jonagold apple   This crisp, juicy apple is a Jonathan-Golden Delicious cross.  It's a bit tart and good for eating out of hand. Jonathan apple   This tart apple is good for eating out of hand, though it has a fairly thick peel.   Lady apple  These are very small apples, and usually used for decoration.
