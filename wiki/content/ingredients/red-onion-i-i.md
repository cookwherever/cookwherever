red onion      These are sweet enough to eat raw, and they're often used to add color to salads.  They're also excellent grilled or lightly cooked.   Varieties include the sweet red Italian onion, Italian red onion, creole onion, and red torpedo onion.  Spanish onion OR green onions (in salads) OR sweet onion OR white onion
