zucker hut = zuckerhut = sugar hat Look for this in German markets. During the Christmas and New Year's holidays, Germans pour rum over the cones and ignite them to make feuerzangebowle, or fire tong punch.

### Other Names

* zucker hut
* zuckerhut
* sugar hat
