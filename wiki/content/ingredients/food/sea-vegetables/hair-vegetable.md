hair vegetable = black moss = hair seaweed = hair-like vegetable = fat choy The Chinese add this to soups and use it as a garnish. Look for it in Chinese markets and pharmacies.

### Other Names

* hair vegetable
* black moss
* hair seaweed
* hair-like vegetable
* fat choy
