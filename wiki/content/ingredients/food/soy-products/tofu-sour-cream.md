tofu sour cream = soy sour cream This made with tofu, and it's lower in fat and more nutritious than ordinary sour cream. Look for it in health food stores. Substitutes: soy yogurt OR soy mayonnaise

### Other Names

* tofu sour cream
* soy sour cream
