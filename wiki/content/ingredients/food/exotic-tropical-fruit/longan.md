longan = lungan = dragon's eyes Longans are very similar to lychees and rambutans. You can buy them fresh (in the summer), dried or canned. Substitutes: lychees (larger and juicier, but not as sweet) OR rambutans

### Other Names

* longan
* lungan
* dragon's eyes
