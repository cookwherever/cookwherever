conchiglioni = jumbo shells = giant shells Pasta shaped like conch shells come in various sizes; this is the largest. It's often stuffed and baked. Substitutes: lumaconi OR manicotti OR cannelloni

### Other Names

* conchiglioni
* jumbo shells
* giant shells
