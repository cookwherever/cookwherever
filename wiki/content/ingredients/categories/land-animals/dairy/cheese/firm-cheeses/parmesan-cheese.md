### Notes
- firm
- pungent and salty
- best is the Northern Italian Parmigiano-Reggiano,

### Substitutes
- grana Padano
- Romano
	- higher in fat
	- sharper flavor
- aged Asiago 
	- saltier
- Sapsago
	- lower in fat
- Swiss Sbrinz
- Fontina
- Monterey jack
- nutritional yeast
	- if sprinkled over a dish
