veal cube steak = veal cubed steak This is a relatively tough cut of meat that the butcher tenderizes by turning it almost into hamburger. Substitutes: beef cubed steak OR ground veal (formed into a patty)

### Other Names

* veal cube steak
* veal cubed steak
