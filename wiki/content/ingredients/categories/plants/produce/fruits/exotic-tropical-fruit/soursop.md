soursop = guanabana sow-ER-sop Notes: This large, dark green fruit is covered with soft prickles. The pulp has a slightly acidic, tropical flavor. Don't eat the seeds or peel. Substitutes: cherimoya (smaller)

### Other Names

* soursop
* guanabana
