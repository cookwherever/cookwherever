shimeji mushroom = pioppini mushroom Like matsutake mushrooms, these grow on trees. They're very tasty, with a peppery flavor. They're great in stir-fries. Substitutes: matsutake mushrooms

### Other Names

* shimeji mushroom
* pioppini mushroom
