lemon balm = balm = melissa = bee balm Cooks use this herb in teas, salads, jams, and soups. The fresh leaves also make an attractive garnish. Substitutes: bergamot (herb) OR lemon zest

### Other Names

* lemon balm
* balm
* melissa
* bee balm
