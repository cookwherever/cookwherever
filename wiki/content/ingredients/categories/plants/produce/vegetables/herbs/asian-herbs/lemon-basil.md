lemon basil = bai maengluk = bai manglak = kemangi This has a lemony flavor, and small, pointed, fuzzy leaves. Thai cooks toss it into soups, salads, and noodle dishes. Substitutes: basil + mint OR sweet basil

### Other Names

* lemon basil
* bai maengluk
* bai manglak
* kemangi
