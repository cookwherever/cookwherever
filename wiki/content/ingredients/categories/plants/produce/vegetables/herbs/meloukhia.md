meloukhia = Jew's mallow = jute = molukhia Middle Eastern cooks use this as an herb in their soups. In other regions, fresh meloukhia is used as a cooking green, much like spinach. Substitutes: spinach

### Other Names

* meloukhia
* Jew's mallow
* jute
* molukhia

* [[middle-eastern]]
