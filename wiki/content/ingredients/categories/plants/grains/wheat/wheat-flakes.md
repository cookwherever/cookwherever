wheat flakes = rolled wheat This is wheat that's been steamed, rolled, and flaked. Wheat flakes are often cooked as a hot cereal, or added raw to granola mixes. Substitutes: triticale flakes OR rolled oats

### Other Names

* wheat flakes
* rolled wheat
