Shanghai noodles = Shanghai-style noodles = pancit Miki = mi xau These thick noodles are often used in stir-fries or soups. Substitutes: perciatelli OR spaghetti

### Other Names

* Shanghai noodles
* Shanghai-style noodles
* pancit Miki
* mi xau
