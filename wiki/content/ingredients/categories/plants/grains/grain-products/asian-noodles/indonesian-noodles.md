Indonesian noodles = mi = mie Indonesians like to use bean threads (which they call su un), and rice vermicelli. They also use egg and wheat-flour noodles to make bami goreng, a fried noodle dish.,

### Other Names

* Indonesian noodles
* mi
* mie
