smooth pasta = pasta lisce This is pasta with a smooth surface. Smooth pasta sometimes has a "lisce" or "lisci" adjective behind the pasta name (e.g., penne lisce or ditali lisci). Substitutes: pasta rigate

### Other Names

* smooth pasta
* pasta lisce
