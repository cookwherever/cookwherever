spaghettini spah-geh-TEE-nee Notes: This is thin type of spaghetti. It works best with light, delicate sauces. Substitutes: vermicelli OR angel hair (thinner) OR spaghetti (thicker) OR rice sticks

### Other Names

* spaghettini
