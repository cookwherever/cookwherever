orzo = rosa marina = rosamarina OR-zoh Notes: This pasta is shaped like grains of barley. It's often used as a bed for sauces or in soups. Substitutes: risi OR rice OR other soup pasta

### Other Names

* orzo
* rosa marina
* rosamarina
