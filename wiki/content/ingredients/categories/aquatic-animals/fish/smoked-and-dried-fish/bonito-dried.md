bonito, dried = katsuobushi = katsuo-bushi Bonito are related to mackerel, and the Japanese dry them and use them in soups. They're often shaved into thin flakes called bonito flakes or hanakatsuo.

### Other Names

* bonito, dried
* katsuobushi
* katsuo-bushi
