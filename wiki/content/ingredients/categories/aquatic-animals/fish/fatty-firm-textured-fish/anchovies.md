### Notes
- Salted is better than canned
- Rinse the salt off
### Substitutes
- anchovy paste, 1/2 teaspoon anchovy paste per fillet
- smelt
- sardines
### Storage
- Unopened canned
	- Year in a dry, cool place
- Opened
	- Two days covered in refrigerated
