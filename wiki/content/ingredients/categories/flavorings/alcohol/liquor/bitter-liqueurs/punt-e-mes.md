Punt è Mes = Punt e Mes POONT eh MESS Notes: This is a bitter form of red vermouth. It's often mixed with soda. Substitutes: Byrrh OR Cynar OR vermouth OR Fernet Branca OR Campari OR Suze

### Other Names

* Punt è Mes
* Punt e Mes
