spatulaspringform pan  These pans are great for baking delicate desserts that are prone to falling apart when removed from the pan.  The rim loosens from the bottom when the clamp is opened.  This pan has two bottoms, so that it can serve as both a tube pan (pictured) and a layer cake pan. tart pan     These often have removable bottoms.
