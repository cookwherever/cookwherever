savory  This herb has a strong, peppery flavor, and it's often used in Mediterranean countries to flavor beans, mushrooms, vegetables, and meats.  There are two varieties:  winter savory   and the milder summer savory.  Winter savory is best suited to slowly cooked dishes like stews.   thyme (stronger flavor) OR thyme + dash of sage or mint
