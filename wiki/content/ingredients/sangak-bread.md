sangak bread = Iranian bread = naneh sangak This Iranian flatbread is about two feet long, enough for the whole family. Substitutes: pita bread OR naansope Notes: Mexican cooks put various savory toppings on these corn patties. Look for them in Hispanic markets. Substitutes: corn tortilla OR gordita
