pheasant    Equivalents:   One large pheasant = 3 pounds.  Notes:   These tend to be pricey, but they're more flavorful than chickens.  One pheasant can serve two people.  Pheasants are lean, so bard them before roasting.   guinea fowl OR Cornish game hens OR chicken (moister, not as gamey) OR grouse OR chukar OR pigeon
