soy sauce Soy sauce is made from soybeans that have been fermented and salted.  It's used throughout Asian, with different regions producing quite different variations.  Japanese soy sauce = shoyu is sweeter and less salty than Chinese soy sauce.  Chinese soy sauce comes in light and dark versions. Lite soy sauce has 1/3 less sodium.    tamari (thicker) OR Maggi seasoning OR black bean sauce thinned with water OR kecap manis (much sweeter) OR Worcestershire sauce
