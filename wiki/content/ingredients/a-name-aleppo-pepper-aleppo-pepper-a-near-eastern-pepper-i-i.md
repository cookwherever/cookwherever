Aleppo pepper = Near Eastern pepper  uh-LEP-oh   Substitutes:  Four parts sweet paprika plus one part cayenne pepper.mahlab = mahlepi = mahleb  (ground kernels of cherry stones) Middle Eastern grocery stores  Chinese almonds, ground OR grind together: 1 2" stick of cinnamon + 3 cloves + 1 bay leaf (Source: Von Welanetz Guide to Ethnic Ingredients) OR ground fennel seeds OR cardamom.
