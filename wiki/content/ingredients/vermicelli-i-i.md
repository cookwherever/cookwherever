vermicelli      ver-mih-CHELL-ee  Notes:   Vermicelli (Italian for "little worms") is similar to spaghetti, only with very thin strands.  Serve it with very light sauces, or break up the rods and serve the pieces in a broth.  fedelini (similar) OR capellini (thinner) OR angel's hair pasta (thinner still) OR spaghettini OR rice sticks
